About Cal-FIT

Welcome to Cal-FIT, your AI-powered nutrition assistant. We believe healthy living begins with understanding what you eat. Our goal is to make nutritional awareness simple, personalized, and smart.

What We Do

Cal-FIT scans food labels and breaks down the nutritional content instantly. It tells you what’s good, what’s risky, and what to avoid — all tailored to your health profile.

Why Choose Us

Most nutrition apps are either too complex or too basic. Cal-FIT bridges that gap with intelligent analysis, a clean design, and personalized health tracking powered by AI and OCR technology.
